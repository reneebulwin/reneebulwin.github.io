Talk Topics

What I Speak About

🔥

Bleed Into Purpose

A signature keynote on turning pain into purpose — speaking openly about trauma, the masks we wear, and the exhausting weight of pretending.

📖

What Was Buried Did Not Stay Lost

Drawing from Lost & Found, this keynote explores how buried truths shape our lives — and how the act of naming them begins the journey back to ourselves.

🤍

Healing Begins When Someone Feels Seen

A talk about the transformative power of being truly witnessed — and how radical honesty can break cycles of silence and shame.

👥

The Masks We Wear to Appear Fine

An honest look at the coping mechanisms we build to survive — the performance of "okay" — and what it takes to lay them down safely.

⚓

Learning to Feel Safe Again

Rebuilding trust within yourself after emotional pain — a grounded, trauma-informed exploration of what safety can look like after it's been taken away.

☀️

Becoming Who You Were Meant to Be

The process of rediscovering identity beyond survival mode — and what it means to stop living as the person trauma made you.

🌿

Strong Too Early

For the ones who had to grow up before they were ready. A compassionate look at what happens when children carry what adults were meant to hold.

✨

Reiki as a Pathway to Restoration

An introduction to Reiki-centered healing and how gentle energy work supports the nervous system's return to calm.
