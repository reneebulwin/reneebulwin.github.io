Speaking Engagements

When I speak, it isn't something I studied from the outside.

Every speaking engagement — whether a keynote, seminar, or workshop — is an invitation not just to understand trauma, but to stop suffering in silence around it.

Talk Topics

  1. 01

    Bled Into Purpose

    A talk about turning pain into purpose — speaking openly about the emotional weight of trauma, the feeling of being discarded, and the masks we wear to appear "fine" before we were ready to say our truth.

  2. 02

    What Was Buried Did Not Stay Lost

    Drawing from Lost & Found, this talk explores how buried truths shape our lives — and how the act of making them visible begins the journey back to ourselves.

  3. 03

    Healing Begins When Someone Feels Seen

    A talk about the power of being truly witnessed — how healing your own unconscious pain can break cycles of silence and create real community.

  4. 04

    The Masks We Wear to Appear Fine

    An honest look at the coping mechanisms we build to survive — the performance of "okay" — and what it takes to lay them down safely.

  5. 05

    Learning to Feel Safe Again

    Rebuilding trust within yourself and your life after trauma — grounded, trauma-informed tools for feeling safe in your own skin again.

  6. 06

    Becoming Who You Were Meant to Be

    The process of rediscovering who you really are — and what it means to step into life as the person you were always meant to be, before the pain tried to decide for you.

  7. 07

    Strong Too Early

    For the ones who had to grow up before they were ready — a compassionate look at what adults owe children who carry what adults were never meant to carry alone.

  8. 08

    Reiki as a Pathway to Restoration

    An introduction to Reiki-centered healing and how gentle, grounding energy supports the nervous system's return to calm — especially for those who believe healing means having all the answers first.
