Her Story

I'm Renée.

A woman who spent years surviving in silence before realizing that silence was never where healing was meant to live.

What I speak about isn't something I studied from the outside. It's something I lived through, carried quietly, questioned, buried, and eventually faced head-on. My journey through childhood trauma, shame, self-worth, and healing became the foundation for something much bigger than pain itself. It became purpose.

In my book Lost & Found, I share that journey in full — the unraveling, the breaking points, and the unexpected discovery waiting on the other side. What once felt like destruction slowly revealed itself as transformation.

The Work

Bleed Into Purpose

Through Bleed Into Purpose, I speak openly about the realities many people are afraid to say out loud — the emotional aftermath of trauma, the feeling of being discarded, the masks we wear to appear "fine," and the exhausting weight of pretending.

My goal is not to speak at people, but to sit beside them in truth, honesty, and understanding.

My voice is for the people who learned how to survive before they ever learned how to live. For the ones who became strong too early. For the ones still carrying stories they were never supposed to carry alone.

"I believe healing begins the moment someone finally feels seen."

— Renée Bulwin

Lost & Found

What Was Buried Did Not Stay Lost.

Some truths don't disappear. They wait. In Lost & Found, Renée Bulwin shares her deeply personal journey of confronting the past she once kept locked away. Through the unraveling of childhood trauma, the weight of silence, and the breaking points that followed, she begins to uncover something unexpected — herself.

What unfolds is more than a story of pain. It is a story of awakening. What once felt like destruction slowly reveals itself as transformation. This is not just about what was lost. It's about what was found in the aftermath.

Speaker Bio

About Renée

Renée Bulwin is the founder and voice behind Bleed Into Purpose, a platform dedicated to speaking openly about childhood trauma, emotional healing, resilience, and personal transformation. Through raw honesty and powerful storytelling, she creates spaces where people feel seen, understood, and empowered to stop suffering in silence. Her mission is to help others turn pain into purpose and reclaim their voice, identity, and strength.
