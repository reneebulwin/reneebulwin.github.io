Her Story

I'm Renee — a woman who spent years surviving in silence before realizing that silence was never where healing was meant to live.

What I speak about isn't something I learned from the outside. It's something I lived through, carried quietly, questioned, buried, and eventually faced head-on. My journey through childhood trauma, shame, self-worth, and healing became the foundation for something much larger than the pain itself. It became purpose.

"In my book Lost & Found, I share the journey in full — the stumbling, the resilience, the way life somehow slowly repairs itself."
"I believe healing begins the moment someone finally feels seen." — Renee Bulwin, Lost & Found

Bleed Into Purpose

Through Bleed Into Purpose, I speak openly about what many people are afraid to say out loud — the feeling of being discarded, the masks we wear to appear "fine," and the exhausting weight of pretending. My goal is not to speak at people, but to sit beside them in truth, honesty, and understanding.

Her Voice Is For

Her voice is for the people who learned how to survive before they ever learned something different. For the ones who became strong too early. For the ones still carrying stories they were never supposed to carry alone.

Speaker Bio

Renee Bulwin is the founder and voice of Bleed Into Purpose, a platform dedicated to speaking openly about childhood trauma, emotional healing, resilience, and personal transformation. Through her speaking, writing, and healing circles, her mission is to help others turn pain into purpose and reclaim their voice, identity, and strength.
