Bleed Into Purpose

What I speak about isn't something I studied from the outside. It's something I lived through, carried quietly, questioned, buried, and eventually faced head-on. My journey through childhood trauma, shame, self-worth, and healing became the foundation for something much larger than the pain itself. It became purpose.

Through Bleed Into Purpose, I speak openly about the realities many people are afraid to say out loud — the emotional aftermath of trauma, the feeling of being discarded, the masks we wear to appear "fine," and the exhausting weight of pretending. My goal is not to speak at people, but to sit beside them in truth, honesty, and understanding.
